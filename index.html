🎒 Useful Expressions & Vocabulary

Take these words and sentence patterns away for your next design pitch!

🎨 1. Colours & Base

📝 Sentence Patterns:
  • The main colour I chose is...
  • It is designed in a classic...
💡 Vocabulary:
vibrant鮮艷的
pastel粉彩/柔和的
neutral中性的
classic經典的

🌟 2. Highlighting Features

📝 Sentence Patterns:
  • One of the key features is...
  • To make it stand out, I included...
💡 Vocabulary:
feature特色
stand out突出/搶眼
unique獨特的
patch布貼/襟章

📍 3. Talking About Locations

📝 Sentence Patterns:
  • It is placed in the centre.
  • You can find it located on the left.
💡 Vocabulary:
centre正中間
corner角落
strap手挽帶
bottom底部

🤔 4. Giving Reasons (Adjectives)

📝 Sentence Patterns:
  • I chose this design because it looks...
  • This design is special to me because...
💡 Vocabulary:
energetic有活力的
creative有創意的
professional專業的
calm平靜的
